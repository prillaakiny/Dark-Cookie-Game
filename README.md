# Dark Cookie Game

Link to site: https://prillaakiny.github.io/Dark-Cookie-Game/

Do you know the annoying banners you receive when you want to access websites? Well, those banners seek your permission to track all your activities online and often sell your personal information. 
It is possible to reject these cookies, but most times people don't know how. This game teaches you how to respond to various types of real-life cookie banners.
